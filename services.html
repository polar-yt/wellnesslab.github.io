Our Services

Reflexology - $70-$80

Reflexology is a complementary therapy that involves the application of pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, and sometimes ears. Based on the principle that these areas correspond to different organs and systems within the body, reflexology aims to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being. This service is available.

Tsuboki Face Massage - $90

The Tsuboki face massage is a rejuvenating treatment that harnesses the power of ancient Japanese healing techniques. By combining acupressure and lymphatic drainage, Tsuboki works to restore balance and harmony to the face and body. Beyond its aesthetic benefits, such as improved skin tone, reduced puffiness, and a natural lift, Tsuboki promotes deep relaxation and stress relief. This service is available.

Scalp Massage - $70

A scalp massage offers a multitude of benefits beyond relaxation. By stimulating blood flow to the scalp, it nourishes hair follicles, potentially promoting hair growth and thickness. Additionally, it helps to relieve tension and headaches, often associated with stress. Regular scalp massage can also improve sleep quality by inducing relaxation. Moreover, it aids in exfoliating the scalp, removing product buildup and dead skin cells, creating a healthier environment for hair growth. This service is available.

Enjoy our services with Young Living Essential Oils™

These services infused with essential oils offer a holistic approach to well-being, combining the physical benefits of massage with the therapeutic properties of aromatic plants. These powerful oils are absorbed through the skin and inhaled, promoting relaxation, reducing stress, and easing muscle tension. Additionally, essential oils can target specific concerns like pain relief, improved sleep, or mood enhancement, creating a truly customized and rejuvenating experience. Services that include essential oils have an extra fee of $10.

Disclaimer for the injured

Please avoid reflexology if you possess the following conditions: Any foot injuries (Open wounds, infections, fractures, etc), advanced diabetes, pregnancy (1st trimester), severe neuropathy, foot fungus, gangrene, advanced osteoporosis, athletes foot, blood clotting, and swelling. Please consult with your physician before booking a reflexology session for your safety and ours as well.
