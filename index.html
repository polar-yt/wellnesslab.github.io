What are essential oils?

Essential oils are concentrated extracts from plants, capturing their essence in liquid form. These aromatic compounds have been used for centuries in various cultures for their potential therapeutic benefits. People believe that essential oils can promote relaxation, reduce stress, improve mood, and even aid in physical well-being when inhaled, applied topically, or diffused.

What is reflexology?

Reflexology is a complementary therapy that involves applying pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears. The theory is that these points correspond to different organs and systems in the body. Reflexology boasts an ancient history, with roots tracing back to ancient Egypt and China. Evidence suggests that early civilizations practiced forms of foot and hand therapy as healing modalities.

What are the benefits of reflexology?

The benefits of reflexology include relaxation, stress relief, improvement of blood circulation, pain relief, improvement of sleep, and the overall enhancement of your bodies well-being and health.
